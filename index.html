A Closer Look

An album release poster for Lou Reed. Handwritten elements within a design.

Handwritten flourishes extend to the book itself! This additional commentary is invaluable to personal feel of the book in contrast to the more direct base text.

Interesting use of sticky-notes with the handwriting to add a journal like feel as well as meta-commentary.

A project that is looked back on none-to-fondly is completely "X'd" out with tape. Obscuring even the content type.

"Concept is Stefan's driving mantra and everything else follows from that --texture, color, typeface, copy, etc."

-Tony Goldman, Goldman Properties, client
